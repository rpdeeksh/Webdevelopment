Our Menu

Neer Dosa

A soft and thin rice crepe from Karnataka, neer dosa is made from a simple batter of soaked rice and water. Its light texture and neutral taste make it a perfect companion for spicy curries like Kundapura chicken or coconut chutney.

Kundapur chicken

This flavorful chicken curry originates from the coastal town of Kundapura in Karnataka. Made with coconut, red chilies, and aromatic spices, it has a rich, mildly spicy, and slightly tangy taste, making it a perfect accompaniment to neer dosa or rice.

Kundapur chicken sukka

A dry variation of Kundapura chicken, this dish is prepared with grated coconut, roasted spices, and ghee, giving it a fragrant and slightly crispy texture. It is a popular Mangalorean delicacy, best enjoyed with neer dosa or chapati.

Chicken Ghee Roast

Ghee Roast is a famous Mangalorean dish known for its rich, aromatic, and spicy flavor. Made by slow-roasting chicken, prawns, or paneer in a generous amount of ghee, it features a fiery red masala prepared with dried red chilies, tamarind, and a blend of roasted spices. Traditionally served with neer dosa, idli, or rice, ghee roast is a must-try for lovers of bold and flavorful Indian cuisine.
